Ahmed Kedir

 

Ahmed Kedir

I am a Ph.D. student in the department of computer science at Norwegian University of Science and Technology, advised by Sule Yildirim Yayilgan, Marius Pedersen and Øistein Hovde . Before that, I received a Master degree from Chonbuk National University (Jeonju, South Korea) in 2014 and a Bachelor degree from Addis Ababa University (Addis Ababa, Ethiopia) in 2009.

My research interests are broadly in artificial intelligence, with emphasis on medical imaging and computer vision. My current work focuses on capsule video endoscopy image and video quality enhancement for improved visual and automatic polyps/cancer and ulcerative colitis detection.
